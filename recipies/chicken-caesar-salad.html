Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

A classic salad combining crisp romaine lettuce, grilled chicken, crunchy croutons, and a creamy garlic dressing, all topped with shaved parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup shaved parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Caesar Dressing

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  2. Grill or pan-sear the chicken over medium heat for about 6 to 7 minutes per side, until fully cooked.
  3. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice it into strips.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, parmesan, lemon juice, garlic, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce to make the dressing.
  5. Toss the chopped romaine with the dressing until evenly coated.
  6. Top the salad with the sliced chicken, croutons, and shaved parmesan.
  7. Serve immediately.
