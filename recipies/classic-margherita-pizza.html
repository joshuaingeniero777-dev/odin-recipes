Classic Margherita Pizza

Description

A simple Italian classic built around three ingredients: fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and basil. The crust is thin and crispy, letting the toppings shine.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, torn into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting, ideally 500°F, with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Stretch the dough into a round shape on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the crushed tomatoes evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Scatter the torn mozzarella pieces across the top.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Carefully transfer the pizza onto the hot stone or sheet and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves before slicing and serving.
